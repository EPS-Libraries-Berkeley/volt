EPS Libraries¶

The Engineering & Physical Sciences Division of the UC Berkeley Library includes five locations. Learn more about the features of each library location below and find them on the UC Berkeley Campus Map. Our libraries all include study space, printing and scanning, public access computers, and course reserves for classes in their subject areas.

Chemistry & Chemical Engineering Library¶

Building Location: 100 Hildebrand Hall
Phone: (510) 642-3753

Features
Power Bank and Calculator Lending
Seminar Rooms 100D, 100E, and 100F
Massage Chair
Chemistry & Chemical Engineering Book and Journal Collections
Librarian: Kristen Greenland

Earth Sciences & Map Library¶

Building Location: 50 McCone Hall
Phone: (510) 642-2997

Features
Reservable Group Study Room
Map Scanning Service
GIS Workstations
Map Collections
Earth Sciences Book and Journal Collections
Librarians: Susan Powell | Sam Teplitzky

Engineering Library¶

Building Location: 110 Bechtel Engineering Center
Phone: (510) 642-3366

Features
Laptop, Power Bank, and Calculator Lending
Reservable Group Study Rooms
Diversity & Inclusion Book Collection
Technical Reports Collection
Engineering Book and Journal Collections
Librarians: Lisa Ngo | Brian Quigley

Mathematics Statistics Library¶

Building Location: 100 Evans Hall
Phone: (510) 642-3381

Features
Power Bank and Calculator Lending
Mathematics and Statistics Book and Journal Collections
Librarian: Brian Quigley

Physics-Astronomy Library¶

Building Location: 351 LeConte Hall
Phone: (510) 642-3122

Features
Physics and Astronomy Book and Journal Collections
Librarians: Kristen Greenland
